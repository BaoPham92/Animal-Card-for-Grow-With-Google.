Raccoon Animal Card!

Ed The Raccoon

Raccoons, just like owls are nocturnal or are mostly active during the night especially during spring, summer and fall.

  • IScientific Name: Procyon lotor
  • Average Size: 12" tall; 24-38" long; 14-23 lbs.
  • Average Lifespan in the Wild: 2-3 years
  • Identifying Features: Gray fur with a black mask and 4-7 black rings around its tail; pointy snout with a black nose; dexterous front paws

Raccoons are awesome. They always just want to hang out and steal your food. I think they are the perfect Netflix to share popcorn with haha.
